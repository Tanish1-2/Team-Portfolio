Our Journey

Who We Are

We are four exceptional students from PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 1, Sagar, united by our passion for artificial intelligence and innovation. Our team came together to participate in the KVS AI Vidyasetu Hackathon 1st Edition 2025-26, a prestigious national competition fostering AI innovation among school students across India.

Our Mission

  • Develop innovative AI solutions addressing real-world challenges
  • Showcase student potential in cutting-edge technology
  • Create meaningful projects that drive positive impact
  • Foster collaboration and peer learning excellence
  • Represent our institution with distinction at national level

What Sets Us Apart

Our team brings diverse technical expertise spanning full-stack development, AI/ML, UI/UX design, and data analysis. Our collaborative approach, innovative thinking, and commitment to excellence secured us 3rd rank in West Zone and qualified us for the national finals.
